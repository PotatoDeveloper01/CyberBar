Welcome to CYBERBAR!

You're the new bartender at this neon-drenched establishment. Your goal is to mix drinks, serve customers, earn cash, and upgrade your bar & skills!

How to Play:

  • Click 'SETUP' then 'OPEN BAR' to start the day.
  • Customers will arrive and order drinks. Their order appears in the chat bubble.
  • Drag ingredients from the list to the Mixing Station.
  • Some drinks require shaking â€“ hit the 'Shake' button!
  • Once the drink is ready, click 'Serve Drink'.
  • Manage your money, unlock new recipes & ingredients, and upgrade your bar from the tabs on the right.
  • Don't let customers wait too long, or they'll leave angry!
